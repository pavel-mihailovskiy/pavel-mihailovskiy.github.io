I’m a senior engineer who owns features and systems end to end — clarifying the problem, shaping the design, shipping tested code, and keeping it healthy in production. I work closely with product and stakeholders, collaborate through thoughtful code review, and prefer clear architecture over clever shortcuts.

How I work

  • Full lifecycle ownership: requirements, estimation, implementation, tests, release, and maintenance.
  • Practical architecture — APIs, background jobs, search, and third-party integrations that hold up under real traffic.
  • Production mindset: CI/CD, monitoring, troubleshooting, and refactoring legacy code without stopping delivery.
  • Partner with business on trade-offs; translate fuzzy needs into shippable increments.
  • Strengthen the team through code review, shared standards, and steady delivery.

I focus on

  • Tested, readable code that others can change safely.
  • Technical direction that balances speed and long-term quality.
  • Systems that solve real user and business problems.
  • Teams that ship reliably together.
